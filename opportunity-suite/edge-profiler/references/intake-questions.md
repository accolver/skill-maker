# Intake Questions

Use these when the user's profile is underspecified. Ask only the fewest needed.

## Core questions

- What specific industries or buyer types do you understand unusually well?
- What warm access do you already have to buyers, operators, or communities?
- What audience or distribution do you control today, and who is actually in it?
- What legal, employer, confidentiality, or licensing boundaries apply?
- How much time and money can you actually commit in the next 30-90 days?

## Clarifying questions

- Are your strongest assets public and reusable, or tied to your employer?
- Are your contacts actual buyers, peers, or just interested followers?
- What work would you refuse to do even if it would make money faster?

## Wedge-discovery questions when niche or distribution is weak

- Who can you reach independently in the next 30 days that could plausibly buy,
  not just chat?
- What specific workflow or recurring pain have you seen repeatedly, and for
  whom?
- Which prior project, consulting engagement, or built app generated the
  strongest inbound, referrals, or repeat requests?
- Can you name 5-20 specific people, companies, or communities you could
  approach without using employer-mediated access?
- Which of your relationships are actual buyers or budget owners versus peers or
  followers?
- What examples, artifacts, or know-how are clean-room safe to reuse?
- Are you willing to start with a narrow paid service, diagnostic, or concierge
  wedge before software? If not, what existing distribution asset replaces that?
