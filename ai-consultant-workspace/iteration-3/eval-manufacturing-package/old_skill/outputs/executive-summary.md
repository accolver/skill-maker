# Executive Summary: AI Opportunity Assessment for TitanWorks

## Situation

TitanWorks is losing $2 million per month — $24 million per year — to unplanned equipment downtime across 4 factories. Only 40% of critical equipment has sensor monitoring; the rest relies on manual inspection. The company recently migrated to Microsoft Azure and has an enthusiastic operations team, but has zero data science capability and is cautious after a previous digital transformation initiative that did not deliver results 18 months ago.

## Key Findings

1. **AI Maturity**: TitanWorks scores **1.7 out of 5.0** on the AI Maturity Model, placing the company in the **Foundation** stage. The primary gaps are in Talent (score: 1 — no data science capability) and Governance (score: 1 — no AI policies or processes). However, the existing sensor infrastructure and Azure cloud platform provide a viable starting point for targeted AI deployment.

2. **Top Opportunities**: We identified 8 AI opportunities across operations, maintenance, and quality. The top 3 by impact-feasibility score are:
   - **Predictive Maintenance on Instrumented Equipment** (Impact: 5, Feasibility: 4): Deploy a SaaS PdM platform on the 40% of equipment that already has sensors. Estimated steady-state benefit: $3.4M/year. Time to first value: 90 days.
   - **Maintenance Knowledge Base (GenAI)** (Impact: 3, Feasibility: 4): Capture tribal knowledge in a conversational AI tool for technicians. Estimated benefit: $700K/year. Time to first value: 60 days.
   - **Sensor Expansion + Full PdM Coverage** (Impact: 5, Feasibility: 2): Instrument remaining 60% of equipment and scale PdM. Estimated incremental benefit: $5.6M/year. Requires Phase 1 success first.

3. **Risks**: The previous failed initiative creates organizational caution that must be addressed directly. The complete absence of data science talent means TitanWorks must rely on SaaS platforms and external partners initially. The CFO's requirement for hard ROI is addressable — the $2M/month baseline makes the business case compelling even under pessimistic assumptions.

## Recommended Approach
