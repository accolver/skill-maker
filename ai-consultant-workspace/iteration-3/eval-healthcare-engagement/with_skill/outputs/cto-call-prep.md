# CTO Call Preparation Guide: MedTech Solutions

## Call Objectives

1. **Validate or refine** the preliminary AI maturity assessment
2. **Understand** the technical landscape (EHR, data, infrastructure)
3. **Confirm or dismiss** red flags (data, expectations, budget, compliance)
4. **Build rapport** and establish yourself as a prepared, credible partner
5. **Align** on engagement scope, timeline, and next steps

## Pre-Call Positioning

You know more about their situation than they expect. Use this strategically:

- Lead with what you've researched (industry context, competitor AI moves)
  rather than asking questions you could have answered yourself
- Show healthcare AI domain expertise early — reference ambient documentation,
  coding AI, and prior auth automation as examples you've seen work in similar
  organizations
- Name-drop specific healthcare AI benchmarks (2-3 hrs/day saved on
  documentation, 30-50% coding error reduction) to demonstrate credibility

## Agenda (Recommended 60 minutes)

### Opening (5 min)
- Brief personal intro and engagement framing
- "I've done some research on MedTech Solutions and the specialty clinic
  landscape. I'd like to share a few observations and then dive into some
  questions to make sure I'm calibrated correctly."

### Your Research Summary (5 min)
Share 2-3 observations from the company briefing to demonstrate preparation:
- "I understand you're running specialty clinics with ~500 employees and $80M
  in revenue. The CEO mentioned paper-based intake as a starting point."
- "In the specialty clinic space, I'm seeing three major AI adoption trends:
  ambient clinical documentation, revenue cycle automation, and patient
  engagement. I want to understand where MedTech fits."
- "You have a hybrid on-prem and AWS environment, which gives us a foundation
  to work with."

### Discovery Questions (35 min)

**Block 1: Strategic Alignment (10 min)**

| # | Question | Why You're Asking |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | What are MedTech's top 3 business priorities this year? | Anchors AI to real goals |
| 2 | When the CEO says "use AI to modernize," what does that mean to you? What does success look like in 12 months? | Tests CTO's vision specificity; validates expectations flag |
| 3 | What's driving the urgency now? | Reveals real motivation and timeline pressure |
| 4 | Is there a budget range allocated, or are we building the case? | Directly addresses budget flag |
| 5 | Who else needs to be involved in this decision? | Maps stakeholder landscape |

**Block 2: Technical Landscape (15 min)**

| # | Question | Why You're Asking |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | What EHR/EMR system(s) do you use? What's adoption like? | Center of healthcare data; biggest unknown |
| 7 | Walk me through the patient data journey — intake through billing. Where's it digital, where's it paper? | Maps actual data landscape |
| 8 | What's on AWS vs. on-prem? What workloads are in the cloud? | Infrastructure maturity |
| 9 | What does your IT team look like? (breakdown of skills/roles) | Talent assessment |
| 10 | Any enterprise AI or analytics tools licensed? (Microsoft 365 w/ Copilot, etc.) | Existing AI investment check |

**Block 3: Risk and Readiness (10 min)**

| # | Question | Why You're Asking |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 11 | Are you aware of staff using AI tools like ChatGPT informally? | Shadow AI risk |
| 12 | Has your compliance team been part of this AI conversation? | Compliance flag |
| 13 | Have you tried any tech modernization projects before? What happened? | Change readiness; past failure risk |
| 14 | How does your organization typically handle change? | Culture assessment |

### Preliminary Findings Preview (10 min)
Based on what you've learned during the call, share:
- "Based on what you've described, I'd place MedTech around a [X] on our AI
  maturity scale. Here's what that means..."
- "I see [X] as a natural first initiative because..."
- "Before we go further into AI solutions, I want to flag [risk] as something
  we should address..."

### Next Steps (5 min)
- Propose the discovery engagement structure (stakeholder interviews)
- Identify who else you need to speak with (department heads, clinic directors,
  billing manager, compliance officer)
- Agree on timeline for next conversations
- Set expectation for a Discovery Report + Roadmap as deliverables

## Scenario Playbook

### If CTO has a specific solution in mind already
- Don't dismiss it. Say: "That's a valid option. Let me do some diligence
  across the full landscape to make sure we're picking the highest-impact
  starting point. Your idea may well be it."
- Investigate their suggestion alongside your opportunity analysis.

### If CTO says "we have no budget yet"
- Shift to: "Let's frame this engagement as building the business case. I'll
  help you build an ROI analysis the CEO can take to the board."
- Propose a smaller-scope AI Readiness Assessment (80-160 hrs, ~$40K-$80K)
  as the first paid engagement.

### If CTO reveals they have a modern EHR with good data
- This significantly changes the maturity score (Data could jump to 2.5-3.0).
- Pivot toward faster opportunities: coding AI and ambient documentation
  become more feasible.
- Ask about EHR vendor's AI offerings (Epic has AI, Oracle Health has AI,
  athenahealth has integrations).

### If CTO reveals significant shadow AI usage
- Treat as urgent: "This is actually a positive signal — your people want
  AI. But we need to get governance around it immediately for HIPAA
  compliance."
- Position shadow AI audit as an immediate deliverable.

### If CTO is technically sophisticated and wants architectural details
- Be ready to discuss: RAG architectures, LLM serving on AWS (Bedrock vs.
  SageMaker), HIPAA-compliant LLM options, BAA-covered AI services.
- Reference AWS HIPAA-eligible services list.

### If there's political tension between CEO and CTO
- Listen carefully for signals of misalignment
- Position yourself as a bridge: "My job is to give you both a shared
  picture of what's realistic and valuable"
- Ensure CTO feels ownership, not just CEO mandate

## Key Metrics to Reference

Use these industry benchmarks in conversation to demonstrate expertise:

| Metric | Benchmark | Source Context |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clinician documentation time | 2-3 hrs/day after hours | Industry average for specialists |
| Coding error reduction with AI | 30-50% | Healthcare coding AI vendor data |
| Prior auth time per case | 15-45 minutes manual | AMA prior auth surveys |
| Patient no-show reduction with AI | 5-15% | Patient engagement AI vendors |
| Claim denial rate (specialty) | 5-25% | Revenue cycle industry benchmarks |
| Time to ROI for digital intake | 3-6 months | Healthcare IT implementation data |

## Post-Call Actions

1. Document all findings within 24 hours
2. Update the AI Maturity Assessment with validated scores
3. Refine red flags based on CTO responses
4. Update opportunity matrix feasibility scores
5. Draft stakeholder interview schedule
6. Send CTO a brief follow-up email summarizing key takeaways and next steps
