# TitanWorks: 3-Tier Investment Proposal for Predictive Maintenance

**Prepared for:** TitanWorks CFO & Executive Leadership Team
**Date:** March 2026
**Classification:** Confidential

---

## Proposal Overview

Based on the maturity assessment and opportunity analysis, we present three investment tiers that offer increasing levels of capability, coverage, and return. Each tier is self-contained and delivers measurable value. Higher tiers build on lower tiers and can be approved incrementally based on demonstrated results.

**The CFO's Key Question Answered:** Every tier pays for itself. The question is not whether to invest, but how fast TitanWorks wants to close the $24M/year gap with its peers.

---

## Tier 1: Essential -- "Stop the Bleeding"

**Objective:** Deploy condition-based monitoring on the highest-failure equipment at 2 factories to prove the model and deliver quick wins.

**Duration:** 6 months
**Scope:** 2 factories (highest downtime), top 30 critical assets

### What's Included

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **SaaS PdM Platform** | 12-month license for condition monitoring on 30 assets |
| **Sensor Deployment** | Vibration, temperature, and current sensors on 30 critical machines (CNC, presses, pumps) |
| **Edge Gateway Installation** | 2 edge gateways (1 per factory) for sensor data aggregation |
| **Basic Dashboards** | Real-time equipment health dashboard for maintenance supervisors |
| **Alert Configuration** | Automated alerts for anomalous vibration, temperature, and current patterns |
| **ERP Integration** | Basic work order generation from alerts (API connection to existing ERP) |
| **Training** | 2-day on-site training for maintenance teams at both factories |
| **Project Management** | Dedicated vendor PM for 6-month implementation |

### Investment

| Cost Item | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| SaaS platform license (Year 1, 30 assets) | $120,000 |
| Sensors + edge hardware + installation | $210,000 |
| Vendor professional services | $120,000 |
| ERP integration | $75,000 |
| Training & change management | $40,000 |
| Internal project management (0.5 FTE, 6 months) | $35,000 |
| Contingency (10%) | $60,000 |
| **Total Tier 1 Investment** | **$660,000** |

### Expected Returns

| Metric | Baseline | Target (Month 6) | Target (Month 12) |
|--------|----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Unplanned downtime (2 factories) | ~$1.1M/month | $935K/month (-15%) | $825K/month (-25%) |
| Mean time to repair (MTTR) | ~7 hours | 5.5 hours | 4.5 hours |
| Emergency parts expediting | $220K/month | $175K/month | $145K/month |
| Planned vs. unplanned ratio | 40:60 | 50:50 | 60:40 |

**Annual Savings (steady state, Month 7-12):** $3.3M - $4.0M across the 2 factories
**Simple Payback Period:** 2.0 - 2.4 months of steady-state savings
**Year 1 ROI:** 280% - 360% (accounting for ramp-up period)

### Key Milestones

| Month | Milestone | Success Criteria |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Vendor selected, contract signed | Vendor meets evaluation criteria |
| 2 | Sensors installed at Factory 1 | 15 assets instrumented, data flowing |
| 3 | Sensors installed at Factory 2 | 15 assets instrumented, data flowing |
| 3 | First alerts generated | System detects at least 3 genuine anomalies |
| 4 | Dashboards live | Maintenance supervisors using daily |
| 5 | ERP integration complete | Automated work orders generating |
| 6 | Tier 1 ROI report | Quantified downtime reduction vs. baseline |

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Vendor platform underperforms | Low | High | 90-day performance clause in contract; pilot on 5 assets first |
| Sensor installation delays | Medium | Medium | Pre-survey all 30 assets; order sensors with 4-week lead time buffer |
| Maintenance team resistance | Medium | Medium | Involve lead technicians in asset selection; training before go-live |
| False alarm fatigue | Medium | High | Tune alert thresholds in Month 2-3; assign alert ownership |

**Duration:** 18 months (starts after Tier 2 Month 6)
**Scope:** Full equipment coverage, custom ML models, digital twins, supply chain integration
**Prerequisite:** Tier 2 delivering measurable results; internal team has 6+ months of experience

### What's Included (Incremental to Tiers 1+2)

| Component | Details |

## Recommendation to CFO

**Start with Tier 1. Make the Tier 2 decision at Month 6 based on real data.**

The $660K Tier 1 investment is less than two weeks of current downtime cost. It provides:
1. Hard evidence of ROI within 90 days (what the CFO asked for)
2. A clear go/no-go decision point at Month 6 for Tier 2
3. Zero new hires required (no long-term commitment)
4. A controlled scope that limits organizational risk

If Tier 1 delivers even half the projected savings ($1.65M/year), it pays for itself in under 5 months and provides the evidence base for Tier 2 approval.

**This is not another "digital transformation." It is a capital investment in maintenance with a measurable financial return, deployed incrementally with clear decision gates.**
