# MedTech Solutions -- CTO Discovery Call Agenda

**Date:** [Next Week - TBD]
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Attendees:** [Your Name], MedTech Solutions CTO
**Objective:** Understand current state, validate assumptions, identify quick wins and strategic priorities

---

## 1. Introductions & Engagement Framing (5 min)

- Brief background on your firm and relevant healthcare experience
- Confirm call objectives: listen, learn, and align on next steps
- Set expectation: this is a discovery conversation, not a sales pitch

## 2. Current State Understanding (20 min)

### IT Landscape
- Walk me through your current infrastructure -- what's on-prem vs. AWS today?
- What EHR/EMR system are you using (if any)? Version? Satisfaction level?
- What are your core clinical and business applications?
- How are patient records currently stored and accessed across clinic locations?
- What's your data backup and disaster recovery posture?

### Patient Intake Process
- Can you walk me through a typical patient journey from scheduling to check-in?
- Where are the biggest bottlenecks or pain points your staff reports?
- How do patients currently fill out intake forms? How is that data entered into your systems?
- What's the average time from patient arrival to being seen?
- How many patient visits per day across all clinics?

### IT Team & Capabilities
- How is the 12-person IT team structured? (infra, apps, security, helpdesk?)
- What's the team's experience with cloud-native development? AI/ML?
- Are there any ongoing modernization projects right now?
- What development methodologies does the team follow?

## 3. Strategic Priorities & Pain Points (15 min)

### CEO's AI Vision
- When the CEO says "use AI to modernize operations," what specific outcomes does he envision?
- Are there particular processes or departments he's most focused on?
- Has the leadership team discussed AI use cases internally? Which ones generated the most interest?
- What does success look like in 6 months? 12 months?

### Business Drivers
- What's driving the urgency to modernize now?
- Are there competitive pressures, regulatory deadlines, or growth plans?
- Are you planning to open new clinic locations?
- What are the top 3 operational challenges keeping you up at night?

### Constraints & Concerns
- What are your biggest concerns about an AI/modernization initiative?
- Are there compliance or regulatory requirements we need to be aware of? (HIPAA, state-specific)
- Have you had any previous modernization attempts that didn't go as planned?
- What's the appetite for change among clinical staff?

## 4. Budget & Timeline Exploration (10 min)

- Has a budget range been discussed for this initiative?
- Is there a fiscal year or budget cycle we should be aware of?
- Are there any grants, incentive programs, or existing vendor contracts that factor in?
- What's the ideal timeline for seeing initial results?
- Is there a board or executive approval process for projects of this scale?

## 5. Next Steps & Alignment (10 min)

- Who else should we include in the discovery process? (CMO, COO, clinic directors?)
- Can we schedule a clinic site visit to observe the intake process firsthand?
- Would you be open to a brief technical assessment of your current infrastructure?
- Propose timeline for delivering a preliminary assessment and roadmap
- Agree on communication cadence and primary points of contact

---

## Pre-Call Research Checklist

- [ ] Review MedTech Solutions website and any public press releases
- [ ] Research their specialty clinic types and patient demographics
- [ ] Check for any public regulatory actions or accreditation info
- [ ] Understand current HIPAA requirements for digital patient intake
- [ ] Research competitor clinics and their digital capabilities
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 relevant case studies from similar healthcare engagements
- [ ] Review AWS healthcare compliance offerings (HIPAA-eligible services)

## Key Signals to Listen For

| Signal | Implication |
|--------|------------|
| CTO is frustrated with paper processes | Strong internal champion for change |
| CTO mentions specific AI tools by name | Has done research, may have preferences |
| CTO is cautious about AI hype | Needs evidence-based, incremental approach |
| Mentions staff resistance to change | Change management will be critical |
| Talks about data silos | Integration/interoperability is a priority |
| References compliance concerns frequently | Security-first approach needed |
| Budget is "TBD" or "we'll figure it out" | Need to help frame ROI to unlock budget |
