# MedTech Solutions -- Discovery Questionnaire

**Purpose:** Comprehensive information gathering to support assessment and roadmap development.
**Instructions:** This questionnaire can be completed asynchronously. Not all questions will apply -- skip any that are not relevant. Honest, candid answers will produce the best recommendations.

---

## Section A: Organization & Strategy

### A1. Business Overview
1. How many clinic locations do you operate today?
2. What medical specialties do your clinics cover?
3. Approximately how many patients do you see per day/week across all locations?
4. What is your growth trajectory? Any plans to add locations or specialties in the next 2 years?
5. Who are your primary competitors, and how do they handle digital patient experiences?

### A2. Strategic Goals
1. What are the top 3 business objectives for the next 12-18 months?
2. Where does technology modernization rank among leadership priorities?
3. Has the organization defined specific KPIs or metrics for the modernization initiative?
4. Are there any board-level mandates or investor expectations around digital transformation?

### A3. Organizational Readiness
1. How would you rate staff comfort with technology on a scale of 1-5? (clinical staff vs. admin staff)
2. Has the organization undergone any significant technology changes in the past 3 years? How did they go?
3. Is there a formal change management process in place?
4. Who are the key decision-makers for technology investments? What's the approval process?

## Section B: Current Technology Landscape

1. Describe your on-premises infrastructure (servers, networking, storage).
2. What AWS services are you currently using? Which region(s)?
3. What percentage of workloads are on-prem vs. cloud today?
4. What is your current network architecture between clinic locations? (VPN, MPLS, SD-WAN?)
5. What is your internet bandwidth at each clinic location?
6. Do you have a disaster recovery plan? When was it last tested?

### B3. Data & Integration
1. Where does patient data reside today? (paper charts, EHR, spreadsheets, etc.)

5. Have you experienced any security incidents or breaches in the past 3 years?
6. Do you have cyber liability insurance?
7. Are there any state-specific healthcare regulations you must comply with beyond HIPAA?

## Section E: AI & Modernization Expectations

### E1. AI Understanding
1. What does "AI" mean to the leadership team? What examples or products have they referenced?
2. Has anyone in the organization experimented with AI tools? (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.)
3. Are there specific processes where leadership believes AI could have the biggest impact?
4. What concerns, if any, does the leadership team have about AI? (job displacement, accuracy, privacy?)

### E2. Desired Outcomes
1. If this initiative is wildly successful in 12 months, what does that look like?
2. What metrics would demonstrate success? (patient wait time, staff hours saved, revenue impact?)
3. Are there any "quick wins" the team has already identified?
4. What would cause this initiative to be considered a failure?

### E3. Budget & Investment
1. Has a budget range been allocated for this initiative?
2. Is the budget for a specific fiscal year, or is it flexible?
3. Is there a preference for CapEx vs. OpEx spending models?
4. Are there existing vendor contracts that may need to be considered?
5. What's the expected ROI timeline? (When does leadership expect to see returns?)
