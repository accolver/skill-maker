# Executive Summary: AI Opportunity Assessment for MedTech Solutions

---

## Situation

MedTech Solutions is a $80M specialty clinic chain seeking to modernize operations through AI. The organization currently relies on paper-based patient intake processes and operates a hybrid IT environment (on-premises + AWS) with a 12-person IT team. The CEO has expressed interest in using AI to modernize operations, and the company is in a strong position to act -- executive sponsorship exists, and budget is available. The question is not "should we adopt AI" but "where should we start and in what order."

## Key Findings

1. **AI Maturity: 1.8 / 5 (Foundation Stage).** MedTech Solutions scores at the foundation level, with the strongest dimension being organizational culture (executive interest) and the weakest being data and governance. This is a common and addressable pattern -- the organization has the will but needs to build the foundation. Paper-based processes are the primary blocker for AI adoption.

2. **Top 3 Opportunities by Impact-Feasibility Score:**

   - **Digital Patient Intake** (Impact: 5, Feasibility: 5): Replace paper forms with digital intake platform. Estimated annual value of $150K-$300K. Deploy in 30-60 days. This is the critical enabler -- it creates the data foundation for all subsequent AI initiatives.

   - **AI Patient Communication Agent** (Impact: 4, Feasibility: 4): Automated appointment reminders, pre/post-visit communication, and routine patient inquiries. Estimated annual value of $200K-$400K. Deploy in 60-90 days. Reduces no-shows by 10-30% and frees front-desk staff.

   - **Ambient Clinical Documentation** (Impact: 5, Feasibility: 3): AI-powered clinical note generation from patient encounters. Estimated annual value of $500K-$1.2M. Requires 3-4 month pilot. Saves clinicians 2-3 hours/day -- the highest single ROI opportunity.

3. **Key Risks:** Budget has not been explicitly defined (medium risk), expectations may exceed near-term achievability (medium-high risk), and data readiness is low (high risk -- addressed by Phase 1 digitization). No critical red flags that would prevent engagement from proceeding.

## Recommended Approach

**Phase 1: Foundation and Quick Wins (Month 1-3) -- Est. Investment: $80K-$180K**
Deploy digital patient intake across all clinics and launch an AI patient communication agent. This digitizes core workflows, creates structured data, and demonstrates immediate value. Estimated annual return: $350K-$700K.

**Phase 2: Clinical AI Pilot (Month 3-6) -- Est. Investment: $100K-$200K**
Pilot ambient clinical documentation with a subset of clinicians. Validate ROI, refine workflows, and prepare for broader rollout. Establish AI governance framework. Estimated annual return: $500K-$1.2M at full scale.

**Phase 3: Scale and Optimize (Month 6-12) -- Est. Investment: $175K-$430K**
Deploy medical coding AI and prior authorization automation, leveraging the digital data foundation from Phases 1-2. Expand clinical documentation to all clinicians. Build internal AI capability. Estimated annual return: $550K-$1.1M (incremental).
