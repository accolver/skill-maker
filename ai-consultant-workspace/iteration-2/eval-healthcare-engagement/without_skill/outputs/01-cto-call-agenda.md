# MedTech Solutions -- CTO Discovery Call Agenda

**Date:** [Next Week -- TBD]
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Attendees:** [Your Name], MedTech Solutions CTO
**Objective:** Understand current state, validate pain points, align on vision and next steps

---

- [ ] Research CTO's background (LinkedIn, conference talks, publications)
- [ ] Review MedTech Solutions website for clinic locations, specialties, public-facing tech

## Agenda

### 1. Introduction & Relationship Building (5 min)

- Brief personal introduction and consulting background
- Acknowledge the CEO conversation and express enthusiasm for the engagement
- Set expectations: "Today is about listening and understanding your world"

### 2. Current State Discovery (20 min)

#### Clinical Operations
- Walk me through the patient journey from scheduling to checkout at one of your clinics.
- Where are the paper-based touchpoints today? (intake forms, consent, clinical notes, billing)
- What EHR/EMR system are you using, if any? Version? Satisfaction level?
- How do patients currently schedule appointments? (phone, portal, walk-in mix)
- What is your current patient volume per clinic per day?

#### Technology Landscape
- Can you describe your current infrastructure? (on-prem servers, AWS usage, network)
- What's your AWS footprint today? (which services, how mature)
- What applications are business-critical? (scheduling, billing, labs, imaging)
- How is data flowing between systems today? Any integration layer (HL7, FHIR, APIs)?
- What's your data backup and disaster recovery posture?

#### IT Team & Capabilities
- How is your 12-person IT team structured? (infra, apps, helpdesk, security)
- What's your team's experience with cloud-native development?
- Any experience with ML/AI projects, even small ones?
- What development methodologies do you follow?
- Key vendor relationships? (EHR vendor, MSP, cloud partner)

### 3. Pain Points & Priorities (15 min)

- What are the top 3 operational pain points the CEO wants solved?
- Where do you lose the most staff time to manual processes?
- What compliance or audit issues keep you up at night?
- Have you had any data breaches or near-misses?
- What's your patient satisfaction feedback telling you?
- If you could fix one thing tomorrow, what would it be?

### 4. Vision & Constraints (10 min)

- When the CEO says "use AI to modernize," what does that mean to you specifically?
- Are there specific AI use cases you've already been thinking about?
- What does success look like in 6 months? 12 months? 3 years?
- What's the appetite for change among clinic staff and physicians?
- Any regulatory concerns or compliance requirements driving urgency? (HIPAA audits, state regs)
- Are there any "third rail" topics -- things that are off-limits or politically sensitive?

### 5. Budget & Timeline Framing (5 min)

- Has a budget range been discussed internally for this initiative?
- Are there fiscal year deadlines or budget cycle considerations?
- Is there a preference for phased investment vs. larger upfront commitment?
- Any existing contracts or vendor obligations that constrain timing?

### 6. Next Steps & Close (5 min)

- Summarize key takeaways and confirm understanding
- Propose next step: on-site clinic visit + stakeholder interviews (1-2 days)
- Offer to send a brief summary of today's conversation within 24 hours
- Identify who else should be involved (CMO, CFO, clinic managers, compliance officer)
- Agree on communication cadence and preferred channels

---

## Questions to Hold in Reserve (if conversation flows naturally)

- How do you handle patient data across clinic locations today?
- What's your HIPAA compliance program look like? (privacy officer, training, audits)
- Have you evaluated any AI vendors or platforms already?
- What's the physician turnover rate? (indicator of workflow friction)
- Are you involved in any value-based care contracts? (drives data needs)
