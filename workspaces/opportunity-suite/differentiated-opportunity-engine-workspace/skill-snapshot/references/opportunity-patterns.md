# Opportunity Patterns

Use this list to generate realistic candidate opportunities. Do not force every
category into the final answer; widen first, then cut aggressively.

## Cash-now patterns

- Productized niche service
- Audit plus implementation offer
- Lead-generation or research service
- Internal workflow automation for a niche
- Reporting or operations concierge
- Data cleanup or enrichment service

## Asset-later patterns

- Micro-SaaS for a narrow workflow pain point
- Data product or benchmark feed
- Template or playbook library with recurring updates
- Niche tooling for compliance, reporting, or ops
- Workflow API or internal tool spun out as a product

## Hybrid ladder patterns

- Service -> automation -> product
- Research service -> data asset -> subscription product
- Done-for-you ops -> dashboard/tool -> SaaS
- Lead-gen service -> internal tooling -> platform
- Manual reporting -> self-serve reporting product

## Content-supported patterns

- Niche newsletter that feeds a service or tool
- SEO content around a pain point that drives software demos
- Educational content that pre-sells consulting or templates

## Anti-patterns

- Generic faceless content farm with no unique angle
- Obvious AI wrapper with no data, distribution, or workflow moat
- Platform-abuse plays that depend on spam or policy loopholes
- Opportunities that need a large audience before first revenue
- Ideas that ignore the user's constraints or current buyer access
