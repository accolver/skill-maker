# Compliance Evidence Concierge Offer

## Positioning

This offer is a **compliance evidence concierge** for small financial firms that
need to stay audit-ready without paying for a full-time compliance operator.

The service does **not** provide legal advice, interpret regulations, or act as
counsel. It helps firms organize, gather, package, and maintain the operational
evidence they already need for internal reviews, client due diligence, and
external audits.

## Ideal Buyer

- Small financial firms with 5-50 people
- RIAs, broker-dealer adjacent firms, fund admins, fintech operators serving
  financial clients, outsourced CFO/accounting firms with compliance obligations
- Founder-led or COO-led teams with limited admin capacity
- Already aware they need cleaner evidence, policies, logs, or review trails
- Prefer a trusted human operator over new software rollout

## Buyer Situation

- They have warm trust-based access to the buyer through referrals or existing
  relationships
- They have limited weekly delivery time, so the offer must stay narrow and
  repeatable
- The buyer wants less scramble before audits, client reviews, or renewal cycles
- The buyer is sensitive to anything that sounds like unauthorized legal or
  regulatory advice

## Core Problem

Small firms often do the work but fail to keep evidence in a clean, reviewable
format. The result is last-minute scrambling, missing artifacts, inconsistent
naming, version confusion, and wasted executive time.

## Promise

"We help small financial firms keep their compliance evidence organized,
current, and easy to produce when someone asks for it."

This promise stays on the operational side:

- evidence collection
- document organization
- review cadence support
- audit-readiness packaging
- owner follow-up and reminders

It avoids:

- legal interpretation
- regulatory conclusions
- certifications or guarantees
- acting as chief compliance officer unless separately qualified and engaged for
  that role

## Offer Structure

### Flagship Offer

**Compliance Evidence Concierge**

A monthly retainer service that maintains a firm's evidence room and prepares
clear evidence packs for recurring requests.

### What Is Included

- Initial evidence inventory of existing documents, logs, and recurring
  obligations
- Simple evidence map by category, owner, source, and refresh cadence
- Standardized folder and file naming structure in the client's existing systems
- Monthly evidence check-in and missing-item follow-up
- Packaging of evidence for a specific event: audit, diligence request, annual
  review, cyber questionnaire, or investor request
- Status summary showing complete items, stale items, and open gaps
- Light project management with internal owners

### Explicitly Not Included

- Legal advice
- Regulatory interpretation
- Drafting legal opinions
- Representing the client before regulators
- Signing off that the firm is compliant
- Building custom software unless separately scoped

## Delivery Model

Keep the delivery model low-overhead and repeatable.

### Phase 1: Evidence Setup Sprint

Time-boxed setup engagement over 2-3 weeks.

Outputs:

- evidence inventory
- evidence room structure
- priority gap list
- refresh calendar
- first packaged evidence binder or request response set

### Phase 2: Lightweight Monthly Retainer

Ongoing concierge support with a narrow cadence.

Outputs:

- monthly evidence maintenance
- owner reminders
- stale-item tracking
- one packaged request set per month or quarter depending on tier

## Recommended Packages

### 1. Evidence Setup Sprint

Best for warm contacts who want to try the service before committing.

- Fixed fee
- 2-3 weeks
- One business unit or one firm-wide baseline inventory
- Ends with a usable evidence map and cleaned repository

Suggested pricing range:

- $3,000-$7,500 depending on complexity and document sprawl

### 2. Essential Concierge

For smaller firms with predictable needs.

- Monthly retainer
- One monthly review cycle
- Async support
- One packaged request set every 1-2 months

Suggested pricing range:

- $1,000-$2,500 per month

### 3. Audit-Ready Concierge

For firms facing regular diligence, annual reviews, or multiple stakeholder
requests.

- Monthly retainer
- Higher-touch follow-up
- Monthly status summary
- One packaged request set per month
- Priority turnaround for evidence requests

Suggested pricing range:

- $2,500-$5,000 per month

## Why This Offer Fits The Constraints

### Warm Contacts

Warm relationships reduce the trust barrier for handling sensitive operational
documentation. The sale can start from a familiar pain point: "You should not
need to hunt for the same files every quarter."

### Limited Weekly Time

The offer is intentionally concierge-style rather than advisory-heavy. It relies
on:

- repeatable templates
- standard evidence categories
- fixed monthly cadence
- async check-ins
- bounded deliverables

This prevents open-ended consulting.

### Avoiding Legal-Advice Framing

The language stays focused on operations, readiness, evidence hygiene, and
coordination. The service supports the client's compliance process without
claiming to decide what the law requires.

## Messaging

### One-Line Description

"We organize and maintain your compliance evidence so audits, diligence
requests, and annual reviews stop becoming fire drills."

### Short Pitch

"Most small financial firms are already doing much of the required work, but the
evidence is scattered across inboxes, drives, and individual team members. We
act as a compliance evidence concierge: we organize the materials, track
refreshes, follow up on missing items, and prepare clean evidence packs when
needed. We support your process operationally and do not provide legal advice."

### Problem-Led Outreach Angles

- "How much time do you lose hunting for the same evidence each quarter?"
- "If a client or auditor asked for your current evidence set this week, how
  ready would you be?"
- "Who owns the follow-up when compliance documentation goes stale?"

## Sales Approach

Start with a diagnostic conversation, not a regulatory discussion.

### Discovery Questions

- What kinds of evidence requests do you get most often?
- Where do documents currently live?
- Which materials go stale most often?
- Who ends up chasing missing items internally?
- What upcoming event would make being organized most valuable?

### Low-Friction Entry Offer

Sell the **Evidence Setup Sprint** first. It is easy to explain, finite, and
produces visible value quickly.

Then convert to retainer if:

- recurring evidence requests exist
- ownership is fragmented
- documents change regularly
- leadership values reduced scramble

## Scope Guardrails

To stay safe and sustainable, every proposal should include language like:

- "Operational support for evidence collection, organization, and maintenance"
- "Client remains responsible for legal, regulatory, and compliance
  determinations"
- "Any policy interpretation or legal review should be handled by the client's
  counsel or qualified compliance leadership"

## Delivery Assets To Standardize

To keep effort low, build a minimal reusable toolkit:

- evidence inventory template
- standard evidence categories checklist
- missing-item tracker
- stale-document review sheet
- monthly status summary template
- evidence request packaging checklist
- proposal and scope language with the legal-advice boundary clearly stated

## Risks

- Buyers may actually want a compliance advisor, not an evidence operator
- Scope creep may pull the work into policy drafting or interpretation
- Sensitive-document handling may require clear security practices
- Some firms may have too little process maturity for a light-touch model

## Risk Mitigations

- Use explicit non-legal operational framing in all materials
- Define included deliverables in concrete terms
- Offer a setup sprint before any broad retainer
- Require a single internal owner on the client side
- Use secure shared folders and access controls agreed with the client

## Best Initial Version Of The Offer

If launching quickly, start with this:

**Offer:** Evidence Setup Sprint for small financial firms\
**Outcome:** a clean, current, easy-to-produce compliance evidence repository\
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks\
**Price:** $4,500 fixed fee\
**Next step after delivery:** optional $1,500-$2,500/month maintenance retainer

This is likely the best initial shape because it is:

- easy to explain to warm contacts
- narrow enough for limited weekly capacity
- clearly operational rather than legal
- valuable even without a long-term commitment

## Recommended Final Offer Statement

"Compliance Evidence Concierge helps small financial firms keep their
documentation organized, current, and ready for audits, diligence requests, and
recurring reviews. We handle the operational work of inventorying, structuring,
tracking, and packaging evidence so your team spends less time scrambling. We
support your compliance workflow, but do not provide legal advice or regulatory
determinations."
