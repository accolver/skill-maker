# Validation Metrics

Use explicit thresholds whenever possible.

## Examples

- 5-10 qualified interviews in 7 days
- 1-3 paid pilots
- 10 waitlist signups from the right buyer type
- 2 checkout attempts
- 3 activated users
- 20 percent click-to-interest rate from a segmented audience send

## Kill criteria examples

- Fewer than 5 qualified conversations after targeted outreach
- No buyer will pay deposit or pilot fee
- Activation fails even after the smallest useful version ships
- Every prospect wants custom work that breaks the wedge
